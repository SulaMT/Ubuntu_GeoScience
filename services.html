Exploration Services

Integrated geological solutions across the mineral exploration lifecycle

Ubuntu Geoscience provides integrated geological expertise across all stages of mineral exploration, from early reconnaissance through to resource definition. The focus is on reducing geological uncertainty and converting field data into actionable exploration targets.

Exploration Workflow

1. Reconnaissance Exploration

Regional-scale geological assessment, lithological mapping, and structural framework interpretation in underexplored terrains.

2. Target Generation

Integration of structural geology, geochemistry, and remote sensing to define high-priority drill targets.

3. Drilling Support

Core logging, structural interpretation, and QAQC support to ensure geological integrity during drilling campaigns.

4. Resource Definition Support

Geological modelling inputs, domain definition, and data validation for resource estimation workflows.

Core Technical Capabilities

  • Structural geology and deformation analysis
  • Geological mapping in Precambrian terranes
  • Geochemical interpretation and anomaly targeting
  • GIS and remote sensing (QGIS / ArcGIS)
  • 3D geological data integration
