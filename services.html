Exploration Services

Integrated geological solutions across the mineral exploration lifecycle

Ubuntu Geoscience provides integrated geological and exploration consulting services across all stages of mineral exploration, from early reconnaissance through to target definition and resource evaluation. The consultancy focuses on reducing geological uncertainty, enhancing prospect value through data-driven interpretation, and transforming legacy datasets into modern exploration models using GIS, remote sensing, geophysics, and geochemical integration. By combining technical field expertise with modern exploration technologies, Ubuntu Geoscience designs efficient, scalable exploration programs tailored to complex and underexplored mineral terrains.

Exploration Workflow

STAGE 01

Reconnaissance Exploration

Field and remote sensing based regional geological mapping, lineament extraction, and rapid prospectivity screening to unlock under-explored frontier terrains.

STAGE 02

Target Generation

Fusing multi-spectral satellite imagery, regional geophysics, and surface geochemistry to isolate high-conviction anomalies and define drill-ready targets.

STAGE 03

Drilling Support

Rigorous drill core logging, systematic structural orientation mapping, and real-time QA/QC to maximize data integrity and optimize ongoing drilling campaigns.

STAGE 04

Resource Definition Support

De-risking downstream resource calculations through strict implementation of industry-standard field sampling protocols, database QA/QC, and structural data validation.
