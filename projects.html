Selected Exploration Projects

Mineral exploration experience across orogenic gold, VMS, and structurally controlled mineral systems.

Galat Sufur South Gold Discovery — Sudan

Contribution to the discovery and advancement of the Galat Sufur South (GSS) orogenic gold system within the Nubian Shield of northeastern Sudan.

  • Structural and alteration mapping
  • Field reconnaissance and target generation
  • Chip sampling program design
  • Geological interpretation of mineralised structures
  • Gold anomaly delineation

Bisha VMS District — Eritrea

Technical involvement in the Bisha volcanogenic massive sulphide (VMS) district from exploration drilling through geological modelling support.

  • Core logging and structural interpretation
  • Exploration drilling support
  • Geological database integration
  • Resource estimation support workflows

Daero Paulos Porphyry Gold System — Eritrea

Structural mapping and geological interpretation within the Asmara Belt targeting porphyry-style mineralisation systems.

  • Structural geology interpretation
  • Alteration mapping
  • Core logging and target evaluation
  • Geological model support

ASGM Evaluation — Sierra Leone

Technical assessment of artisanal and small-scale gold mining (ASGM) operations focused on geological controls, mineralisation style, and field-scale evaluation.

  • Field evaluation of artisanal workings
  • Geological assessment of mineralised zones
  • Structural interpretation
  • Regional exploration potential review
