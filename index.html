Mineral Exploration across the Nubian Precambrian Shield

Ubuntu Geoscience: your geology bridge to discovery in the Arabian‑Nubian Shield

Ubuntu GeoScience was founded by MT Menghisteab, an exploration geologist with close to 20 years of exploration experience in diverse mineral deposits types and project leadership across East and West Africa. We specialise in the Arabian‑Nubian Shield and study this Neoproterozoic Shield and its mineralization.

Guided by the Ubuntu philosophy – “Integration” – we believe that deposit discovery emerges from integration: between geologists, data, and careful planning of exploration programs grounded in field and diverse data synthesis. Our approach is field‑first and data‑driven.

We deliver practical, field‑grounded geological expertise that turns prospects into discoveries and advances projects toward development. Whether you need geological mapping, remote sensing reconnesaiance survey, target generation, or exploration project management, Ubuntu Geoscience will provice the technical managerial input.

Key Experience Highlights

  • Key contributor to the discovery of the Wadi Dom prospect (Galat Sufur, Sudan) – a significant orogenic gold discovery.
  • Extensive gold exploration across the Nubian Shield: Ariab, Gabgaba, Dongola corridors.
  • Technical role in the Bisha VMS district (Eritrea) – from exploration drilling to resource estimation.
  • Structural mapping and core logging of the Daero Paulos Porphyry Gold System, Asmara Belt.
  • Artisanal and small‑scale gold mining (ASGM) assessment in Sierra Leone.
