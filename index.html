Mineral Exploration across the Nubian Precambrian Shield

Ubuntu Geoscience: your geology bridge to discovery in the Arabian‑Nubian Shield

Ubuntu GeoScience was founded by Mussie T. Menghisteab, an exploration geologist with more than 15 years of hands‑on experience in mineral exploration and project leadership across East and West Africa. We specialise in the Arabian‑Nubian Shield and analogous Precambrian terrains.

Guided by the Ubuntu philosophy – “I am because we are” – we believe that real discovery emerges from deep collaboration: between geologists, data, and local communities. Our approach is field‑first and data‑driven, ensuring every decision is anchored in outcrop reality.

We deliver practical, field‑grounded geological expertise that turns prospects into discoveries and advances projects toward development. Whether you need geological mapping, remote sensing reconnesaiance survey, target generation, or exploration project management, Ubuntu Geoscience will provice the technical managerial expertise.

Key Experience Highlights

  • Key contributor to the discovery of the Wadi Dom prospect (Galat Sufur, Sudan) – a significant orogenic gold discovery.
  • Extensive gold exploration across the Nubian Shield: Ariab, Gabgaba, Dongola corridors.
  • Technical role in the Bisha VMS district (Eritrea) – from exploration drilling to resource estimation.
  • Structural mapping and core logging of the Daero Paulos Porphyry Gold System, Asmara Belt.
  • Artisanal and small‑scale gold mining (ASGM) assessment in Sierra Leone.
