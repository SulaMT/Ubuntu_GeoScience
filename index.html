Technical Exploration Consultancy

Ubuntu Geoscience delivers integrated mineral exploration services across East Africa, the Arabian-Nubian Shield, and frontier terrains. The consultancy combines field geology, structural interpretation, GIS integration, and exploration management to advance projects from reconnaissance through drill targeting and resource definition.

Experience includes gold, VMS, and porphyry systems across Sudan, Eritrea, and West Africa, including contribution to the Galat Sufur South orogenic gold discovery and technical work within the Bisha VMS district.
