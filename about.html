Technical Leadership & Field Experience

Combining technical field expertise with modern GIS and remote sensing methodologies for reliable target generation and reduce risk and uncertainty.

Ubuntu Geoscience delivers integrated mineral exploration and geoscience consulting services across frontier and underexplored terrains. With a strong operational focus on the Arabian-Nubian Shield, the consultancy combines field geology, geophysics, geochemistry, remote sensing, and GIS-based data integration to transform legacy datasets into modern, data-driven exploration models. Leveraging experience in structurally controlled gold systems, regional-scale targeting, and prospectivity analysis, Ubuntu Geoscience supports clients from early-stage reconnaissance through advanced target generation and technical evaluation.

Exploration Lifecycle Support

1. Reconnaissance Stage

Regional-scale geological assessment, tectonic corridor analysis, terrain evaluation, and early-stage prospectivity screening.

2. Target Generation Stage

Integration of regional geochemistry, geophysics structural mapping, and remote sensing imagery to define robust drill targets.

3. Drilling Stage

On-site geological supervision, rigorous core logging, oriented structural measurements, and QAQC management during critical campaign rollouts.

4. Resource Definition Stage

Geological modeling inputs, detailed structural structural control analysis, and spatial data preparation for grade estimation workflows.
