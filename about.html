Technical Leadership & Field Experience

Grounded in structural observation, remote sensing workflows, and regional discoveries.

Ubuntu Geoscience provides professional mineral exploration and consulting services tailored to frontier terrains. With an operational footprint focused tightly on the complex geology of the Arabian-Nubian Shield, the consultancy bridges the gap between legacy datasets and modern, data-driven prospectivity modeling.

Exploration Lifecycle Support

1. Reconnaissance Stage

Regional-scale geological assessment, tectonic corridor analysis, terrain evaluation, and early-stage prospectivity screening.

2. Target Generation Stage

Integration of regional geochemistry, geophysics structural mapping, and remote sensing imagery to define robust drill targets.

3. Drilling Stage

On-site geological supervision, rigorous core logging, oriented structural measurements, and QAQC management during critical campaign rollouts.

4. Resource Definition Stage

Geological modeling inputs, detailed structural structural control analysis, and spatial data preparation for grade estimation workflows.
