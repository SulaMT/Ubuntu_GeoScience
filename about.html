Exploration Geologist with Over 15 Years of Discovery Experience

Ubuntu: your technical bridge to discovery in the Arabian-Nubian Shield

Ubuntu GeoScience was founded by MT Menghisteab and Colleagues , an exploration geologist with more than 15 years of hands-on experience in mineral exploration and project leadership across East and West Africa.

Guided by the Ubuntu philosophy and a field-based and data-driven exploration approach, we believe that real discovery comes from the deep interconnection between field observations, geological understanding, and integrated data.

At Ubuntu Geoscience, we deliver practical, field-grounded geological expertise that turns prospects into discoveries and advances projects toward development.

Key Experience Highlights

  • Key contributor to the discovery of the Wadi Dom prospect in the B14 Gold Project at Galat Sufur, Sudan.
  • Extensive orogenic gold exploration across the Nubian Shield in northern Sudan (Ariab – Gabgaba – Dongola corridor).
  • Technical involvement in the Bisha VMS district in Eritrea, supporting deposit advancement from exploration drilling through to resource estimation.
  • Structural mapping and core logging of the Daero Paulos Porphyry Gold System in the Asmara Belt, Eritrea.
  • Geological assessment of artisanal and small-scale gold mining projects in Sierra Leone.
